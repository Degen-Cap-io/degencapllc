About Degen Capital

Degen Capital is a leading investment manager specializing in alternative assets. We emphasize an opportunistic, value-oriented and risk-controlled approach to investing in public and private equity and credit. Industries of focus include technology, media, healthcare, business services, and energy. To date, we have invested in excess of $500 million.

Investing Philosophy

Degen Capital employs a rigorous bottom-up fundamental approach to diligence.

Core Tenets

  • Intrinsic value & margin of safety.
  • Business quality & durable moats.
  • Rational, aligned management & governance.
  • Patience & concentration; let winners compound.

Decision Factors

  • Asymmetric risk–reward.
  • Understand mispricing.
  • Multi-year horizon; low turnover; tax-aware.
  • Exit on thesis break or superior alternatives.
